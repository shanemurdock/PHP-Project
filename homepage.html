    About Us


    Crestfield Medical is the leading and longest established private clinic in Ireland. Since it opened in the mid 1980s, the Clinic has consistently built an unparalleled reputation in new high-tech surgical procedures, medical treatments and ground-breaking diagnostics. This reputation means that some of the best international and national consultants work at Crestfield Medical. Patient outcomes at Crestfield Medical are positive. We follow the progress of patients from the beginning of their journey with us and many of our outcomes outstrip international standards.
